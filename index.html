About Me

Core Skills

iOS Development

I have 3+ years experience in iOS development. Moreover, I never stop following latest Cocoa Touch framework and Foundation. In my private project, I try to use those new features as much as possible.

Swift

My development life has been going with Swift. Since its major launch, I've been pursuing possibility of this incredible language. I continuously keep on watching where it's approaching, and keep in mind to write concise and maintainable code.

Libraries

I am used to using open source library. Plus, I realize that a library abuse is next to risk that a project gets uncontrollable. Therefore I also take care of its choice taking their credibility and maintainability into account.



Tools

Continuous Learning

I'm surrounded with the greatest pleasure when learning technologies I like. This is my instinct nobody isn't able to interfere with, and I regard this characteristic as one of my strongest point.

Steadiness

Truth be told, I'm not a super hacker who can solve every technical problem in a moment. Alternatively, I have a strong will eager to fulfill both our team's and clients' expectations. Now I'm ready to consecutively perform very well.
